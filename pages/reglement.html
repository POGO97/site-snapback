SnapBack Reglement

Welcome to SnapBack RP server! We're excited to have you join our community. Below are the rules and regulations that all players must follow:

General Rules

  • Respect all players and staff members.
  • No discrimination, harassment, or hate speech of any kind.
  • Do not exploit glitches or bugs in the game.
  • Follow the instructions of staff members at all times.

Roleplay Guidelines

  • Stay in character at all times while in-game.
  • Use appropriate roleplay names and avoid using real-life names.
  • Do not metagame or powergame.
  • Use /me and /do commands for roleplay actions and interactions.
Server-Specific Rules
  • No random deathmatching (RDM).
  • No trolling or griefing other players.
  • Respect the server economy and do not engage in unrealistic wealth accumulation.
  • Report any rule violations or issues to staff members promptly.
Penalties for Violations

Violation of any of the above rules may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on the severity of the offense and the player's history.
